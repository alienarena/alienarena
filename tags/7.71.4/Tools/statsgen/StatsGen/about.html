ALIEN ARENA GLOBAL STATS
 
Welcome to the new Alien Arena Global Stats page!

This stats program is completely based on game standings vs other ranked players.  It does not matter who you kill, or how you kill them.  If you want to improve your standings, just play to win.

Playing CTF will not affect your rankings either way.  Your frag count will go up, as will your frags per hour, but everything is based on percentages, so it's all relative to the game you are involved with.

You accumulate points in several algorithms.  The first, and foremost, is your frags per minute in the game is calculated against other players you are playing on the server.  You get awarded points for players frag percentages, divided by their current ranking.  In otherwords, you get more points for being ahead of good players rather than bad.  If you are dominating the match, rest assured, your points will come too, as you will get points based on the percentage of frags you have vs total frags in the game as well.

In order to prevent people from just playing alot to pick up cheap points, your point totals are divided by the time you have spent playing the game.

As time goes on, we will be adding new features and new interesting ways to track your progress, so stay tuned!
